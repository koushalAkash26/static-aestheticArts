Aesthetic Arts

Anu carries on the illustrious tradition with a modern twist. Her paintings are wonderful quality, her technique exciting and her love of the subject matter shines through.

Everyone has feelings and there are many beautiful ways to express them, ART is my way of expressing my feelings and one can’t deny the fact that it's beautiful “says Anunitha”

Born and raised in the hills of Nilgiris, Anunitha’s passion for art began at a young age who was inspired by her father’s sketches. Unlike most of them she has a knack for painting aesthetic illustrations and just went with the flow. When it comes to art it's important not to hide the madness inside you and she makes sure of it by diving into all mediums of art.

She sees beauty and peace in nature and thrives to spread positivity through her paintings.

A remarkable contemporary artist that demonstrates the enormous breadth of talent that combines the traditional values of painting with a modern flair.
